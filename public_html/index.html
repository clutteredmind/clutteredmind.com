I am largely self-taught.

While I did take a couple of C++ classes at local colleges years ago, I never "went to college" and I don't have a degree in anything. Eventually, I found my way into IT and spent a number of years building and fixing computers. Over many years and through lots of independent study, I was slowly able to turn "I know something about computers" into "I know how to program!" A few lucky breaks later, I became a professional programmer. And that's where I am today.
